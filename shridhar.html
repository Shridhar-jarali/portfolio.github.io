Salary Distribution Analysis - SDG Mapping

SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

How it's addressed: Analyzing salary distributions can promote fair wages and highlight disparities, fostering inclusive and sustainable economic growth. It helps ensure fair compensation across industries and regions.

SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities

How it's addressed: By identifying income disparities between different demographic groups, locations, or industries, salary analysis directly supports efforts to reduce inequalities within and among populations.

Market Imbalance Detection - SDG Mapping

SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

How it's addressed: Detecting imbalances between job supply and demand helps optimize labor markets, ensuring better employment opportunities and promoting sustainable economic growth.

SDG 4: Quality Education

How it's addressed: Insights from imbalance detection can reveal gaps in skills or training, guiding the development of education and training programs to align with market needs.

SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

How it's addressed:By addressing job market inefficiencies, this analysis supports innovation in workforce planning and strengthens economic infrastructure.
