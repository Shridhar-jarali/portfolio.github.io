Job-Candidate Matching System - SDG Mapping

SDG 1: No Poverty

How it's addressed: Helping job seekers find employment opportunities aligned with their skills contributes directly to reducing poverty levels.

SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

How it's addressed:By leveraging technology for efficient job-candidate matching, the system fosters innovation in workforce planning and strengthens economic infrastructure.

SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

How it's addressed:The system facilitates better alignment between job seekers and employers, reducing unemployment and underemployment while supporting inclusive and sustainable economic growth.

Resume parsing - SDG Mapping

SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

How it's addressed: Resume parsing accelerates the recruitment process, ensuring timely matching of job seekers to suitable positions, thus fostering productive employment and sustainable economic growth.

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

How it's addressed: By enabling local governments and organizations to analyze workforce data, resume parsing helps build resilient and inclusive communities through targeted employment opportunities and skill development programs.

SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals

How it's addressed:Resume parsing technologies can facilitate partnerships between educational institutions, employers, and policymakers by providing valuable insights into skills demand and supply.
